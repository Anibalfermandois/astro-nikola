At Nikola, we're committed to making sustainable energy accessible to every homeowner. Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through innovative solar solutions that are both efficient and affordable.

## Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize home energy solutions, Nikola has been at the forefront of residential solar installations since our inception. Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience in solar technology and installation.

## Our Expertise

- **Certified Professionals**: Our team is certified by the North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners (NABCEP)
- **Quality Assurance**: We use only top-tier solar panels and equipment from leading manufacturers
- **Custom Solutions**: Every installation is tailored to your home's specific needs and energy requirements
- **Comprehensive Service**: From initial consultation to post-installation support, we're with you every step of the way
